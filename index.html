South-Korean singer-songwriter, record producer and actress

IU (아이유)

  • Debuted on September 18, 2008.

  • IU symbolizes people can become one through music.

  • IU is the artist with most number-one songs in South Korea.

  • IU earned the title of "Best New Actress" at 2014 KBS Drama Awards.

MAMAMOO (마마무)

South-Korean girl group by RBW

MAMAMOO (마마무)

  • Members: Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, Hwasa.

  • Debuted with "Mr. Ambiguous" on June 18, 2014.

  • Recognized for their strong vocal and live performances.

  • The "Four Seasons, Four Colors" project showcases each member's style.

G.E.M. (邓紫棋)

Chinese (HK) singer-songwriter, record producer and rapper

G.E.M. (邓紫棋)

  • G.E.M. stands for Get Everybody Moving.

  • First female Chinese singer to exceed 100 million views on YouTube.

  • G.E.M. gained tremendous popularity in China after appearing in the "I Am a Singer" program.

  • "Light Years Away (光年之外)" is released as the theme song for the film "Passengers".

Canadian singer-songwriter, record producer, musician and actor

Henry Lau
